\outcome{Compute Taylor polynomials.}

\tag{power series}
\tag{taylor polynomial}


Find the Taylor polynomial of degree $4$ of $f(x)=\sqrt{x}$ at $c=1$.

\[
\begin{prompt}
p_{4}(x)=\answer{1+\frac{1}{2}(x-1)-\frac{1}{8}(x-1)^2+\frac{1}{16}(x-1)^3-\frac{5}{128}(x-1)^4}
\end{prompt}
\]
