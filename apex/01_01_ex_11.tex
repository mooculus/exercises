Let $f(x) = -7x+2$ and $a=3$. Observe the table of values for $\frac{f({a+h})-f({a})}{h}$:
\begin{center}
 \begin{tabular}{r@{.}lc}
  \multicolumn{2}{c}{$h$} & $\frac{f(a+h)-f(a)}{h}$\\ \hline 
  $-0$ & $1$ & $-7$ \\
  $-0$ & $01$ & $-7$ \\
  $0$ & $01$ & $-7$ \\
  $0$ & $1$ & $-7$
 \end{tabular}
\end{center}
Estimate the limit as $h\to 0$: $\begin{prompt}\answer{-7}\end{prompt}$
