\tag{Stokes' Theorem}

Let $D$ be the portion of $z=2x+5y$ inside $x^2+y^2=1$, oriented upwards, and let $\vec{F}(x,y,z)=\vector{y,z,-x}$. Compute $\int_{\partial D}\vec{F}\dotp\d\vec{r}$

\[
\int_{\partial D}\vec{F}\dotp\d\vec{r}=\answer{-4\pi}
