\tag{Divergence Theorem}

Let $S$ be the boundary of the volume described by $x^2+y^2\le 1$, $0\le z\le 4$. Let $\vec{F}(x,y,z)=\vector{xy^2,yz,x^2 z}$.
Compute $\iint_{S}\vec{F}\dotp\vec{N}\d S$.
