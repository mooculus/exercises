\tag{Stokes' Theorem}

Let $D$ be any surface and let $\vec{F}(x,y,z)=\vector{P(x),Q(y),R(z)}$ ($P$ depends only on $x$, $Q$ only on $y$, and $R$ only on $z$). Compute $\int_{\partial D}\vec{F}\dot\d\vec{r}$.
