\outcome{Take derivatives of compositions of functions using the chain rule.}
\outcome{Recognize a composition of functions.}
The \textbf{derivative} of the function $f$ is given and a table of values for $g(x)$ and $g'(x)$ is shown below.

Suppose that $g$ is a one-to-one function and $g^{-1}(x)$ is its inverse.
\[
f'(x)=\frac{x}{x^2+1},\hskip 1cm
\begin{tchart}{c|c|c}
x & g(x) & g'(x)\\ \hline
1 & 2 & 4\\ \hline
2 & 3 & 5\\ \hline
3 & 4 & 1
\end{tchart}
\]

$\frac{d}{dx}f(g(x))$ at $x=1$ is
\begin{prompt}
\begin{multipleChoice}
\choice{$2$}
\choice{$\frac{2}{5}$}
\choice[correct]{$\frac{8}{5}$}
\choice{$1$}
\choice{$0$}
\choice{None of the previous answers}
\end{multipleChoice}
\end{prompt}
